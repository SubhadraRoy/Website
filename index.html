About Me

Dr. Subhadra Roy is currently working as Associate Professor in Zoology at Surendranath College Kolkata, affiliated to the University of Calcutta. She was awarded the prestigious Satish and Tulsi Manna Memorial Endowment Bronze Medal‚Äù for achieving First Class First position in M. Sc. Zoology under the University of Kalyani. Dr. Roy is also member of the Indian Science Congress Association and the Zoological Society, Kolkata. She has to her credit research papers in Indian and foreign journals and has also authored a textbook on Non-Chordate Zoology.
